02 / 05 — The Practice & Identity

Beyond
the
binary.

Trained as a structural engineer, now working at the intersection of fashion, performance, and installation. Queer and non-binary, the practice refuses the categories fashion uses to contain us — no gender, no season, no trend.

Every garment is zero waste by design. Materials have multiple lives, and the history of each piece matters as much as what it becomes. Based between Barcelona and London. Clients include Legs On The Wall, Ace Hotel Sydney, and LA Design Week.

The Approach

Clothes are not male or female. They are material, form, and intention. Every piece I make is designed to be worn by any body — refusing the binary structures the fashion industry uses to sell us things we don't need.

Same principles.
Bigger reach.

rewear grew directly from the design practice — the same obsession with what materials are, where they come from, and how long they last. The difference is scale. A garment reaches one person. An app reaches everyone.

Built with React Native, Supabase, and a genuine belief that knowing more about what you own changes how you wear it.
