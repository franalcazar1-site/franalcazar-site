02 / 05 — The Practice

Structure
meets
surplus.

Trained as a structural engineer, now working at the intersection of fashion, performance, and installation. The practice is built on a single principle: materials have multiple lives, and the history of each piece matters as much as what it becomes next.

Based between Barcelona and London. Every garment is zero waste by design. Clients include Legs On The Wall, Ace Hotel Sydney, and LA Design Week.

02 / 03 — The Story

Same principles.
Bigger reach.

rewear grew directly from the design practice — the same obsession with what materials are, where they come from, and how long they last. The difference is scale. A garment reaches one person. An app reaches everyone.

Built with React Native, Supabase, and a genuine belief that knowing more about what you own changes how you wear it.
